Beyond Blue Zones: Toward Inclusive Health Archives

This exhibition demonstrates how contemporary health documentation can challenge dominant longevity narratives while preserving diverse ways of knowing about wellness and community care.

By centering participant voices and using community-controlled metadata, we create archives that serve both current communities and future researchers seeking to understand how people actually lived, cared for each other, and created health in the early 21st century.

The future of longevity research depends on inclusive, rigorous documentation of diverse health experiences happening right now.
